How to Use This Guide

This workbook helps you prepare for CELPIP Speaking. It is designed for learners aiming for CLB 5 or CLB 7.

Why These 10 Topics?

I chose these topics because they are the most common themes in CELPIP Speaking tests. From my experience as a CELPIP instructor, you will see these topics in most real tests. This gives you the best preparation.

Each topic gives you two word lists, example sentences, useful connectors, and a real CELPIP-style speaking task.

Practice Routine:

Choose a topic, study the words, practice with the examples, then record a 60-second answer using 2 or 3 new words and at least one connector.

Important Notes

Speak naturally and spontaneously. Do not memorize full answers.

Vocabulary alone does not guarantee your score. CELPIP speaking is evaluated in four areas:

1) Task Fulfillment

You answer the question fully and clearly.

2) Coherence and Organization

Your ideas are connected and easy to follow.

3) Vocabulary

Your words are accurate, appropriate, and varied.

4) Listenability

Your pace, pronunciation, and grammar are clear.
